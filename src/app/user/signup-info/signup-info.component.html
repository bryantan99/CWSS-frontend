Steps to Register a CHIS account

  1. Please fill up necessary details.
  2. If the form is valid, it will be submitted. Otherwise, an error notification will be displayed. Please ensure that your form is valid.
  3. If the form is submitted successfully, please schedule an appointment with the admin to review your submission. Once everything is verified, admin will approve your submission and activate your account.
